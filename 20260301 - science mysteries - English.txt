Nobody knows what dark matter actually is.

You might have heard that most of the universe is invisible.

That is not a metaphor or an exaggeration.

Scientists have determined through the careful observation of how galaxies move

It exerts gravity. It affects the large scale structure of the cosmos and it
has never once been directly detected or observed.

We know dark matter exists because of what it does, but we have no idea what it
is made of. It could be exotic particles that interact with ordinary matter
only through gravity. It could be something even stranger,
something that does not fit into any of our current models of physics.

For decades, experiments have searched for dark matter in underground
laboratories, in data from particle accelerators, in signals from space.

So far, nothing. The majority of the matter in the universe remains completely
unknown to us, as quietly present as something sleeping just out of sight.

Quantum entanglement has no satisfying explanation.

Here is something extraordinary. Two particles can become entangled.

Meaning their properties are linked in such a way that measuring one instantly

No matter how far apart they are, not after a delay, not at the speed of light,

It happens. It is not a measurement artifact or a misunderstanding.

Two particles genuinely do seem to be connected across any distance in a way

Quantum mechanics describes how entanglement works mathematically.

We do not fully understand how anesthesia works.

The universe appears finely tuned for life and we do not know why.

the fundamental constants of the universe.

Things like the strength of gravity, the mass of electrons,

and some see in finetuning an argument for a designer of some kind.
